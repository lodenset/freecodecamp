Here's a time line of Richard P. Feynman life:

  • 1918 - Born in New York City on the 11th May
  • 1939 - He studied at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology
  • 1942 - He studied at the Princeton University
  • 1940-1941 - Research Assistant at Princeton
  • 1945-1950 - Professor of Theoretical Physics at Cornell University
  • 1950-1959 - Visiting Professor and thereafter appointed Professor of Theoretical Physics at the California Institute of Technology
  • 1965 - He was elected a foreign member of the Royal Society, London (Great Britain)
  • 1988 - Died on February 15
